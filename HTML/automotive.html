Top Facts About the Corvette ZR1

  1. The ZR1 is a high-performance variant of the C6 Corvette, featuring:
    • A 6.2L supercharged V8 engine producing 638 hp
    • Carbon ceramic brakes for superior stopping power
    • A top speed of 205 mph
  2. The ZR1 has an advanced active handling system to keep the car stable at high speeds.
  3. It features lightweight carbon fiber body panels to improve performance and efficiency.
